# New Ship Types - Player Guide

VOID RIFT now features **9 unique ships** across different categories, each with specialized roles and playstyles.

## Original Ships (6)

### Vanguard Mk.I - Balanced Strike Fighter
**Role**: All-Rounder  
**Best For**: Beginners, balanced gameplay  
**Strengths**: No weaknesses, reliable in all situations  
**Stats**: All 1.0x (perfectly balanced)

### Phantom-X - Speed Interceptor
**Role**: High-Speed Scout  
**Best For**: Hit-and-run tactics, dodging  
**Strengths**: Extreme speed and boost  
**Weaknesses**: Fragile (0.85x HP)  
**Stats**: 1.22x Speed, 1.28x Boost

### Bulwark-7 - Siege Tank
**Role**: Heavy Assault  
**Best For**: Tanking damage, sustained fire  
**Strengths**: High HP, large ammo capacity  
**Weaknesses**: Slow (0.86x speed)  
**Stats**: 1.35x HP, 1.22x Ammo

### Emberwing - Glass Cannon
**Role**: Damage Dealer  
**Best For**: Maximum firepower, risk-takers  
**Strengths**: Highest damage output  
**Weaknesses**: Low HP and ammo  
**Stats**: 1.28x Damage, 0.9x HP

### Spectre-9 - Stealth Scout
**Role**: Reconnaissance  
**Best For**: Speed demons, collectors  
**Strengths**: Fastest ship, best pickup range  
**Weaknesses**: Very low HP (0.75x)  
**Stats**: 1.35x Speed, 1.4x Boost, 1.25x Pickup

### Titan Heavy - Capital Gunship
**Role**: Heavy Weapons Platform  
**Best For**: Overwhelming firepower  
**Strengths**: Massive HP and damage  
**Weaknesses**: Very slow  
**Stats**: 1.6x HP, 1.35x Damage, 1.45x Ammo

## Choosing Your Ship

### For Beginners
1. **Vanguard Mk.I** - Learn the basics with balanced stats
2. **Bulwark-7** - Forgives mistakes with high HP
3. **Nexus Carrier** - Support role eases difficulty

### For Experienced Players
1. **Wraith Stealth** - Master movement and timing
2. **Phantom-X** - Speed demon gameplay
3. **Emberwing** - Maximum damage with high risk

### For Specific Goals
- **High Score**: Titan Heavy (firepower) or Devastator (area clear)
- **Survival**: Bulwark-7 or Devastator (HP tanks)
- **Speed Run**: Wraith Stealth or Spectre-9 (mobility)
- **Resource Farming**: Nexus Carrier or Spectre-9 (pickup range)

## Mastery Tips

### General Ship Mastery
1. **Learn Your Ship**: Each has unique feel with inertia physics
2. **Upgrade Synergy**: Match upgrades to ship strengths
3. **Weapon Match**: Choose weapons that complement your ship
4. **Know Limits**: Understand your weaknesses
5. **Practice Movement**: Master the banking and inertia

### Advanced Techniques
- **Boost Management**: Save boost for escapes or repositioning
- **Strafe Shooting**: Use momentum to slide while firing
- **Reverse Thrust**: Quick deceleration for tight turns
- **Banking Advantage**: Tilt for visual awareness in 3D mode
