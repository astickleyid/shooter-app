Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Progress saves automatically after each session. Avoid uninstalling the app as this clears local data.

How do I connect to Game Center?

Sign into your Apple ID and Game Center in iOS Settings. Void Rift detects it automatically and submits scores to the leaderboard.

How do I unlock ships and weapons?

Collect credits by defeating enemies. Visit the Ship Hangar from the main menu to spend credits on hulls, weapons, defense systems, and ultimates.

The game isn't loading

Close and reopen the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall. Note: reinstalling resets local progress.

How do I delete my account?

Email us with your username and we will delete your account data within 7 days.
