VOID RIFT

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Void Rift ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Void Rift iOS application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The App collects minimal information necessary to provide core gameplay features:

  • Game Progress & Settings: Your ship configuration, high scores, unlocked items, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device except as described below.
  • Game Center Data: If you sign into Apple Game Center, your Game Center player ID and leaderboard scores are submitted to Apple's Game Center service, governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.
  • Optional Account (Username & Score): If you choose to create a Void Rift account to appear on global leaderboards, we store a username and your high scores. No email address or real name is required. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, or contact information unless you voluntarily provide it.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell data to third parties.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.

How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your game progress on your device.
  • To display global leaderboards if you opt into an account.
  • To submit leaderboard scores to Apple Game Center if you use that feature.

Data Storage & Security

Local game data is stored securely on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. If you create a Void Rift account, your username and scores are stored on our servers with standard security practices. We do not share this information with any third parties except as required to operate the leaderboard service.

Children's Privacy

Void Rift is not directed to children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 4. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center — Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Our leaderboard API is hosted on Vercel, subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may delete your local game data at any time by uninstalling the App. To request deletion of any account data stored on our servers, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted at this URL.
